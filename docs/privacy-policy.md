# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-07-03
**App:** Beacon ("the App")
**Provider:** Nakodan Capital LLC ("we," "us," "our")
**Contact:** beacontour1@outlook.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information Beacon collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

## 1. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the App.

**Account information.** When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and, where provided, your name. We use this to create and identify your account.

**Account identifier.** We assign your account a user ID, used to associate your trips, preferences, and photos with you.

**Content you create.** This includes the destinations you enter, your travel preferences (interests, budget, pace), trips you save, and any photos and captions you add to your Passport. Photos you add are stored on our backend and are private to your account.

**We do NOT collect:**
- Precise or coarse device location. Destinations are text you type; the App does not access your device's GPS.
- Phone number, physical/mailing address, or payment/financial information.
- Health, fitness, contacts, browsing history, or sensitive personal information.
- Analytics or product-usage tracking. We do not embed advertising, analytics, or cross-app tracking SDKs.
- Device advertising identifiers (IDFA) or similar.

We do **not** track you across other apps or websites, and we do **not** sell your personal information.

## 2. How we use information

We use the information above to:
- Authenticate you and maintain your account.
- Generate your itineraries and show them on a map.
- Save and display your trips, preferences, and Passport photos.
- Respond to support requests and keep the App secure and working.

## 3. Third-party services

We use the following service providers to operate the App. They process data only to provide their service to us:

- **Supabase** — authentication, database, and photo storage (our backend).
- **Google** — Places, Routes, and Maps services. When you search a destination or generate an itinerary, the place text and itinerary parameters are sent to Google to return location, routing, and map data. See Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
- **A third-party AI provider** — to generate itineraries, the destination and preferences you enter are sent to an AI language-model service that returns a suggested plan. We do not send your name, email, or photos to this service.
- **Apple / Google Sign-In** — to authenticate you. They handle the sign-in and return your email (and optionally name) to us.

We do not control these providers' own data practices; please review their privacy policies.

## 4. Data sharing

We share information only:
- With the service providers in Section 3, to operate the App.
- If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, where the receiving party honors this Policy.

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

## 5. Data retention

We keep your account information, trips, preferences, and photos for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account or your content, we delete the associated data from our systems, subject to backups and legal requirements, within a reasonable period.

## 6. Your choices and rights

- **Access and deletion.** You can delete photos and trips in the App. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at beacontour1@outlook.com or use the in-app account deletion option.
- **Region-specific rights.** Depending on where you live (e.g., EEA/UK under GDPR, California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these, contact us at the email above. We do not "sell" or "share" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

## 7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, access controls, authenticated storage) to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. We rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers where required.

## 10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. We will post the new version with an updated effective date, and where required, notify you in the App.
